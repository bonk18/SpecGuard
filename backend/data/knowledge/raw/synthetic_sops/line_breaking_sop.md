# Synthetic Line Breaking Procedure

> SYNTHETIC PROTOTYPE SOP — NOT FOR REAL INDUSTRIAL USE

## Purpose

Give a prototype checklist for opening a process line, flange, drain, or other boundary where containment may be released.

## Preconditions

- The line-breaking scope, equipment identity, contents, and isolation boundary are confirmed.
- Required line-breaking and other permits are active, and a competent supervisor is present.
- Depressurization, draining, purging, containment, PPE, and exclusion arrangements are reviewed.

## Required checks

Use the approved verification method before loosening the boundary. Monitor for pressure, liquid, vapor, flammable gas, toxic gas, and unexpected flow. Keep nonessential workers outside the controlled area.

## Suspension or stop-work conditions

Stop immediately for pressure, unexpected odor or release, rising LEL, high H2S, incomplete isolation, loss of ventilation, or changed work conditions. Withdraw and escalate according to the site plan.

## Human approvals and limitations

Operations and safety authorities decide whether the line is safe to open and whether work may restart. This is synthetic demonstration content, not a universal procedure.
