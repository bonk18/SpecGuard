# Synthetic Emergency Evacuation Procedure

> SYNTHETIC PROTOTYPE SOP — NOT FOR REAL INDUSTRIAL USE

## Purpose

Provide a prototype response outline for a suspected fire, explosion risk, toxic-gas release, or other escalating refinery maintenance condition.

## Immediate response

Stop work when directed, warn nearby workers without creating additional exposure, and move by the designated route to the approved assembly area. Raise the site alarm and contact the emergency response organization using established channels.

## Required checks

Account for workers, contractors, and visitors. Share the known location, equipment, observed hazards, and last known conditions with the response coordinator. Do not re-enter to retrieve equipment or investigate without authorization.

## Human approvals and restart

Only the incident commander or designated site authority may declare an area safe, approve re-entry, or authorize restart after investigation and required permits.

## Limitations

This synthetic outline is not an emergency plan, does not replace site alarms or muster arrangements, and is not for real industrial use.
